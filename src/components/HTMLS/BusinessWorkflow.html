Procurement Business Process Workflow

Complete business process flow from RFP creation to goods delivery

Complete Procurement Workflow
1
User / Procurement Lead
RFP Creation
User creates a Request for Proposal with detailed requirements, delivery location, and timeline.
Actions:
  • Fill RFP details and line items
  • Answer product-specific questions
  • Set delivery location and dates
  • Add estimated budget
  • Submit for approval
Outputs:
RFP Draft Status: DRAFT
2
Procurement Manager
RFP Review & Approval
Procurement Manager reviews the RFP for completeness, budget alignment, and business requirements.
Actions:
  • Review RFP details and requirements
  • Validate budget and timeline
  • Approve or reject with comments
  • Add conversation notes if needed
Outputs:
Approval Decision Status: APPROVED/REJECTED
3
System / Procurement Manager
Vendor Selection & Invitation
System matches vendors based on keywords and certifications. Manager selects and invites relevant vendors.
Actions:
  • Auto-match vendors by keywords
  • Filter by certifications/location
  • Send RFP invitations to selected vendors
  • Set quotation cutoff date
Outputs:
Vendor Invitations Status: SENT_TO_VENDORS
4
Vendor
Quotation Submission
Vendors review RFP requirements and submit detailed quotations with pricing, specifications, and documents.
Actions:
  • Review RFP line items
  • Provide technical specifications
  • Submit pricing (excl/incl GST)
  • Upload supporting documents
  • Lock quotation before cutoff
Outputs:
Quotation Supporting Docs Status: SUBMITTED
5
Procurement Manager
Quotation Evaluation
Compare quotations based on price, quality, delivery timeline, and vendor credentials. Shortlist top 3 vendors.
Actions:
  • Compare pricing and specifications
  • Evaluate vendor credentials
  • Check delivery timelines
  • Shortlist top 3 vendors
  • Select preferred vendor
Outputs:
Evaluation Report Vendor Selection Status: VENDOR_SELECTED
6
Finance Manager
Financial Approval
Finance team reviews selected quotation for budget compliance and payment terms before PO generation.
Actions:
  • Review selected quotation amount
  • Verify budget availability
  • Check payment terms
  • Approve or reject selection
Outputs:
Finance Approval Budget Allocation
7
Finance Executive
Purchase Order Generation
Generate formal Purchase Order with terms, conditions, and delivery requirements. Send to selected vendor.
Actions:
  • Generate PO from quotation
  • Add terms and conditions
  • Set penalty clauses
  • Send PO to vendor (Email + SMS)
  • Wait for vendor acknowledgment (48hrs)
Outputs:
Purchase Order PDF Document Status: SENT_TO_VENDOR
8
Vendor
Goods Delivery
Vendor acknowledges PO, delivers goods as per timeline, and uploads invoice to the portal.
Actions:
  • Acknowledge PO within 48 hours
  • Procure and prepare goods
  • Deliver to specified location
  • Upload invoice to portal
  • Provide delivery confirmation
Outputs:
Delivered Goods Invoice Status: DELIVERED
9
Admin Team / User
Goods Receipt & Verification
Admin team receives goods, performs quality checks, and marks receipt in the system. User provides feedback.
Actions:
  • Inspect delivered goods
  • Verify against PO specifications
  • Perform quality checks
  • Mark goods received in portal
  • Provide vendor rating and feedback
Outputs:
Goods Receipt Note Quality Report Vendor Rating Status: COMPLETED

Role Responsibilities
System Admin
  • Manage backend and frontend system
  • Configure product categories
  • Approve new vendor registrations
  • Manage user roles and permissions
Procurement Manager
  • Review and approve/reject RFPs
  • Evaluate submitted quotations
  • Select vendors and shortlist top 3
  • Make final procurement decisions
Procurement Lead
  • Create RFPs with requirements
  • Send RFPs to selected vendors
  • Manage vendor communications
  • Monitor quotation submissions
Finance Team
  • Review financial approvals
  • Generate Purchase Orders
  • Process invoice payments
  • Manage budget allocations
Admin Team
  • Mark goods received in portal
  • Perform quality inspections
  • Manage delivery confirmations
  • Coordinate with vendors
Vendor
  • Register company and products
  • Submit detailed quotations
  • Deliver goods per PO terms
  • Upload invoices and documents
